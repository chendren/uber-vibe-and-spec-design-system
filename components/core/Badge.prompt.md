Small pill label for status, category, level, or module tags.

```jsx
<Badge variant="teal" mono>SPEC</Badge>
<Badge variant="clay" mono>VIBE</Badge>
<Badge variant="success" dot>Enrolling</Badge>
<Badge variant="neutral">Beginner</Badge>
```

Use `mono` for spec-style uppercase tags (LIVE, BETA, MODULE 03). `dot` adds a status dot. Variants map to the brand ramps (`clay`, `teal`) and semantic states (`success`, `warning`, `danger`), plus `neutral` and `solid` (filled clay).
